"Fake currency detection systems help differentiate between counterfeit and real money. Counterfeit currency is imitation money made without legal authority. These systems address challenges faced by people and institutions in identifying fake currency. The project utilizes digital image processing to analyze and process images."

Steps to use the application

  1. Open Application.
  2. Select the algorithm:
    a.CNN
    b.SVC
    c.RFC
  3. Choose the method for data input:
    a.Use Camera
    b.Upload from device
  4. a.If you use camera click clear image and upload.
    b.If you use upload from device , upload clean and clear image.
  5. Click verify and view result.
  6. Note:
    -If result is genuine but percentage is less than 20 of both sides combine then, the note could be counterfiet.
    -If result is counterfiet but percentage is less than 20 of both sides combine then, the note could be real.
